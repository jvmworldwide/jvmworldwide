Frequently Asked Questions

Our quick answers to common inquiries.

What is an e-visa and how it does work?

‚àí

An e-visa is an electronic version of a visa that allows travelers to apply online without visiting an embassy or consulate. With BVS Global, the process is simplified, we help you fill out the application, upload necessary documents, and submit it through the official portal of the destination country. Once approved, the visa is sent via email and linked to your passport electronically. You simply print it out or show the digital copy at immigration when you travel.

What is the difference between a tourist visa and a family visa?

+

A tourist visa is for individuals traveling for leisure, sightseeing, or recreational purposes for a limited period. A family visa, on the other hand, is specifically for individuals who wish to join family members (spouses, children, parents, etc.) who are residents or citizens in another country, often for an extended stay or permanent relocation.

How long will it take to process a travel visa?

+

Visa processing times vary significantly depending on the destination country, the type of visa, and the applicant's nationality. It can range from a few days for some e-visas to several weeks or even months for certain long-term or complex visas. We recommend checking the specific requirements and estimated processing times for your desired visa.

Do I require travel insurance for a tourist visa?

+

Many countries, especially those in the Schengen area, require mandatory travel insurance for tourist visa applications. This insurance typically needs to cover medical emergencies, hospitalization, and repatriation expenses. It's always advisable to have travel insurance for any international trip, regardless of whether it's a visa requirement.

What are the common requirements for a visa application?

+

Common requirements usually include a valid passport (with sufficient validity beyond your intended stay), completed application form, recent passport-sized photographs, proof of financial means to cover your stay, travel itinerary (flight bookings, accommodation), and sometimes an invitation letter or other supporting documents depending on the visa type and country.

What should I do if my visa application is rejected?

+

If your visa application is rejected, you should first understand the reason for the rejection, which is usually provided in a refusal letter. Depending on the reason, you might be able to appeal the decision, reapply with corrected information or additional documents, or consider applying for a different type of visa or to a different country. Our consultants can help you analyze the rejection and advise on the best course of action.

Can I track my visa application status?

+

Most embassies and consulates provide online portals or services where you can track the status of your visa application using a reference number. We will guide you on how to track your application and keep you updated on its progress.

How can I check if I need a visa for my destination?

‚àí

To check if you need a visa for your destination, you can contact BVS Global directly or use our visa eligibility tools. Requirements may vary based on your nationality, travel purpose, and destination. We will inform you whether you need a visa, the type required, and the documents needed. Our team makes sure you have the right information before you plan your travel.
